SNAG-View

SNAG-View is an enterprise solution for system monitoring based on the open source projects Nagios, OTRS and NeDi. In late 2009 the producing companies SECTOR NORD AG and Freicon AG decided to build a new version of the software from scratch. Already involved in the development of earlier versions, I was contracted to manage the project itself and assist in the conception and implementation of the software.

The project was implemented by a team of over 10 people, including developers, system consultants and additional external staff. During the project's realisation phase agile development methods were introduced and combined with the usage of continious integration and delivery.

Within the software itself a variety of different techniques and methods are applied. It has a multi-tier architecture, utilises NoSQL and relational databases, uses open standard webservices as well as a proprietary message protocol and offers a powerful API. Amongst others a dynamic and extensive user rights management, a flexible data definition language, a complex ORM mechanism, different visualisation techniques and a rule-based, failsafe notification system were designed and implemented.

By the end of 2012 the software reached a state which allowed the producers to begin with the roll out. During this period the task of managing the software‘s further development was handed over smoothly to a software developer at SECTOR NORD AG.
